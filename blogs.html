  • Moments and Deviations is a blog devoted to fun and interesting problems in Computer Science and Mathematics for the purpose of keeping me continually learning new ideas and practicing existing skills. If you have interesting problems, feel free to send those to me and I shall post them (with perhaps a solution). Currently, the plan is to post an update about once a week (probably on the weekend).

  • The Hamete Go Group is a blog devoted to studying the game Go. I created this blog so I could still be a part of the awesome Portland Go community despite having moved away. This blog is new so I cannot predict the direction in which this blog will eventually proceed, but so far I am quite excited. If this is something you are interested in beyond just reading (i.e., posting Go games), send me an email and I will give you author-privileges on the blog.
